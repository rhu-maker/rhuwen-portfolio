About Me

I’m a Front-End Developer who loves building the parts of the internet that people see and touch. My journey into tech started with a simple curiosity about how things work. That curiosity turned into a career where I get to turn big ideas into clean, beautiful websites.

While some people like working "behind the scenes" with databases, I’m at my best when I’m working on the user experience. I believe that code should be simple, and using an app should be easy. I spend my time making sure every pixel is in the right place and every interaction feels smooth.

For me, it’s not just about writing code; it’s about making a difference by building tools that are helpful, fast, and great to look at.
